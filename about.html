About

Lokopkarak Pharmaceutical Works is a GMP certified company which was founded by Late Shree Kiran Chandra Jain in 1930 in order to promote ayurveda and to provide people with high quality ayurvedic medicines. His traditional knowledge and experience has served to enhance the quality of the products and services we offer to our customers.


We are the premier organization to formulate the products in the key health segments. Our medicines are prepared using best quality herbs and strictly follow the techniques as described in the ancient Indian literature. This approach is combined with latest technology, meticulous manufacturing process and maintenance of unparallel quality control to achieve zero deviation from quality and consistency with each batch. Care is taken to see that the preparation adheres strictly to the norms and procedures laid out in the ancient books of ayurveda


We posses sufficient infrastructure encompassing all essential machines and equipments along with adequate storage space for raw material and finish goods.


All of our high tech machines that are used in manufacturing medicines are completely environment friendly. Cutting edge facilities and trained man power ensure that we can handle all the requirements of our customer in a capable manner.


We have been awarded GOLD MEDAL for our best quality medicines.
