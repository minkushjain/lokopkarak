Welcome to Lokopkarak Pharmaceutical Works

Lokopkarak Pharmaceutical Works is a GMP certified company which was founded by Late Shree Kiran Chandra Jain in 1930 in order to promote ayurveda and to provide people with high quality ayurvedic medicines. His traditional knowledge and experience has served to enhance the quality of the products and services we offer to our customers.

About Ayurveda

Ayurveda is a wholistic system of medicine from India that uses a constitutional model. Its aim is to provide guidance regarding food and lifestyle so that healthy people can stay healthy and folks with health challenges can improve their health. Ayurveda is made up of two Sanskrit words: Ayu which means life and Veda which means the knowledge of. . According to the ancient Ayurvedic scholar Charaka, "ayu" is comprised of four essential parts. The combination of mind, body, senses and the soul.



G.M.P. CERTIFICATION
